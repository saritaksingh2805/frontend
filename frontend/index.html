  • About our Project

    Our Project On Image Steganography Hiding information by using another information as a cover.

    Image to Text Steganography- It is a method of concealing textual information within the pixels of an image. Image to Text steganography is employed for secure communication, where messages can be concealed within images to protect sensitive information during transmission. Image to Image Steganography- It is a technique of concealing one image, known as the secret image, within the pixels of another image, referred to as the cover image. The goal is to embed the secret image seamlessly into the cover image while maintaining the visual quality of the cover image as much as possible.
